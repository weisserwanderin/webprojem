About Pattisserie


Tradition since 1867

Whether traditional doilies or modern cupcake moulds, praline cases, colourful tulip muffin wraps or decorations: The name Cake's Sweet stands for a wide range of high-quality, innovative paper products that meet the needs of the bakery, patisserie and catering industries. The “Made in Germany” premium quality of our 150-year-old family company impresses our customers worldwide, from industry to the end user.


Established in 1867 in Berlin, we still manufacture and produce on site at our factory and use this base to cater to customers across the world. Our customers include commercial chains, wholesalers and retailers, the confectionery industry, bakeries, the hotel and catering industries as well as private households. Equipped with the latest production technology, we deliver our products in large or small packaging for retailers and private use depending on the customer’s requirements.
Personalised customer service and innovative products

We have always been driven by two principles : one being the close relationship to our customers and the other, the ambition and enthusiasm to continue creating new, innovative products of the highest quality. These products are modern, in line with market requirements and tailored to the customer. We are closely engaged with the market and observe the trends and the constantly changing requirements.


In order to satisfy the specific requirements of our customers, our product team works closely with them as part of a creative exchange. Thanks to the latest production facilities, we can implement customised designs and products developed in our own facility. Our years of experience make us a reliable partner. seasonal ingredients.
